# A03 Phase 5 Blocked - PK/PD Content

## Phase

Phase 5 - PK/PD.

## Status

Blocked.

## Reason

Phase 5 requires source-derived pharmacokinetic and pharmacodynamic values. Structural PK and PD shells exist from A03-024 and A03-025, but content extraction has not been authorized.

## Required Before Execution

- scientific extraction protocol;
- source-bound field extraction;
- field-level traceability;
- validation criteria;
- no dosing guidance or prescribing behavior.

## Next Dependency

`A03-031 - SCIENTIFIC_EXTRACTION_PROTOCOL`

## Declaration Final

Phase 5 cannot be executed as content population until extraction governance is approved.

## Gate Decision

Phase 3 Sprint 1 is accepted as structural knowledge architecture.

The gate does not authorize immediate execution of Phases 4 through 9 as content population.

## Reason

Phases 4, 5, 6 and 7 are marked as `BLOCKED FOR CONTENT` in `docs/PROGRAM_A03_PROGRESS.md`.

Executing them would require scientific extraction from the corpus, field-level source binding, validation criteria and publication rules. Those are not equivalent to the structural shells completed in A03-021 through A03-030.

## Authorized Outcome

The correct next step is to create a controlled scientific extraction protocol before populating content-bearing fields.

## Blocked Phases

- Phase 4 - Mechanisms
- Phase 5 - PK/PD
- Phase 6 - Safety
- Phase 7 - Evidence Integration
- Phase 8 - Knowledge QA
- Phase 9 - Publication

## Safety Boundaries Preserved

- no recommendation;
- no prescription;
- no runtime consumption;
- no dose guidance;
- no safety alert;
- no evidence grade;
- no clinical rule.

## Next Authorized Mission

`A03-031 - SCIENTIFIC_EXTRACTION_PROTOCOL`

## Declaration Final

The A03 Phase 3 baseline is accepted structurally, but Phases 4 through 9 remain blocked until a scientific extraction protocol is created and approved.

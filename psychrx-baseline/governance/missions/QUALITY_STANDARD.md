# A03 Knowledge Quality Standard

## Objective

Define the minimum quality requirements for future A03 knowledge.

## Minimum Completeness

Every future knowledge field must define:

- source;
- version;
- traceability ID;
- review status;
- publication status;
- conflict status.

## Review Gates

- administrative validation;
- scientific review;
- semantic review;
- editorial approval;
- publication gate.

## Readiness States

- draft;
- awaiting validation;
- validated;
- deprecated;
- conflicting evidence.

## Declaration Final

No A03 field is publishable without source, traceability and review.

# C06 - Reporting Execution

## Status

Executed as product and UX governance artifact.

## Scope

C06 defines reporting boundaries for summarizing the consultation workspace.

## Result

Reports may organize clinician-entered information, selected symptoms, stability state, monitoring plan and follow-up plan as documentation support.

## Restrictions Preserved

- Reports are not prescriptions.
- Reports are not autonomous clinical advice.
- Reports do not create new clinical claims.

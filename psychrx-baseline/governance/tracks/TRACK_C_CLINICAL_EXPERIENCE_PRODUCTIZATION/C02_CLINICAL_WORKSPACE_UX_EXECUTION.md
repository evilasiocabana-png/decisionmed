# C02 - Clinical Workspace UX Execution

## Status

Executed as product and UX governance artifact.

## Scope

C02 defines the Clinical Workspace UX as a widget-based environment rather than a collection of disconnected pages.

## Result

The workspace should organize Patient, Symptoms, Clinical Stability, Current Medication, Risks, Objectives, Strategy Options, Monitoring and Follow-up without making conduct decisions.

## Restrictions Preserved

- UI does not decide clinical conduct.
- Widgets are presentation and organization units.
- Clinical decisions remain with the physician.

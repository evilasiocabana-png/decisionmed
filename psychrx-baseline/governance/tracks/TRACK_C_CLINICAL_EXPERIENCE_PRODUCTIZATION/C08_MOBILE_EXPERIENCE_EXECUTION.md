# C08 - Mobile Experience Execution

## Status

Executed as product and UX governance artifact.

## Scope

C08 defines mobile experience principles for the Clinical Workspace.

## Result

Mobile presentation should use stacked clinical widgets, preserve read-only safety status, avoid overloaded tables and prioritize touch-first symptom, stability and follow-up flows.

## Restrictions Preserved

- No mobile-only clinical decision logic.
- No prescription or recommendation controls.
- No runtime clinical activation.

# B06 - Clinical Decision Boundary Execution

## Status

Executed as governance artifact.

## Scope

B06 defines the boundary between organizing clinical reasoning and making autonomous clinical decisions.

## Result

PsychRx remains a clinical reasoning support system. The physician remains responsible for diagnosis, treatment and prescribing decisions.

## Restrictions Preserved

- No autonomous clinical decision was enabled.
- No prescribing or dose selection was enabled.
- No patient-specific medication selection was enabled.

# D05 - Regulatory Readiness Execution

## Status

Executed as validation governance artifact.

## Scope

D05 defines regulatory readiness checkpoints for future review.

## Result

Regulatory readiness requires traceability, intended-use clarity, safety boundaries, change history, validation evidence and explicit non-claim language.

## Restrictions Preserved

- No regulatory approval is claimed.
- No compliance certification is claimed.
- No clinical deployment is authorized.

# Track D - Validation and Certification Execution Plan

## Status

Completed as validation and certification governance execution.

## Purpose

Track D defines the validation and certification layer for PsychRx.

It verifies scientific governance, clinical boundaries, traceability, quality assurance, regulatory readiness, certification readiness, external review and release validation without enabling clinical runtime.

## Programs

### D01 - Scientific Validation

Purpose: define validation requirements for scientific knowledge packages.

### D02 - Clinical Validation

Purpose: define validation requirements for clinical safety boundaries and clinician-controlled use.

### D03 - Traceability Audit

Purpose: define audit requirements from source to published artifact.

### D04 - Quality Assurance

Purpose: define QA expectations for documentation, JSON, tests and governance consistency.

### D05 - Regulatory Readiness

Purpose: define a readiness checklist for future regulatory review without claiming compliance.

### D06 - Certification Readiness

Purpose: define readiness criteria for future internal and external certification.

### D07 - External Review

Purpose: define boundaries and artifacts required for future external scientific, clinical and technical review.

### D08 - Release Validation

Purpose: define release validation requirements before any public or clinical-facing release.

## Permanent Restrictions

- No clinical runtime activation.
- No prescribing.
- No therapeutic recommendation.
- No dose suggestion.
- No autonomous diagnosis.
- No patient-specific medication selection.
- No patient-facing clinical guidance.
- No regulatory compliance claim.
- No release certification claim.

## Declaration

Track D D01 through D08 have been executed as validation and certification governance artifacts. No clinical or regulatory certification is granted by this track.

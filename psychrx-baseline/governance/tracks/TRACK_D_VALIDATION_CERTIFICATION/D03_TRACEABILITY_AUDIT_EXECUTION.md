# D03 - Traceability Audit Execution

## Status

Executed as validation governance artifact.

## Scope

D03 defines traceability audit requirements.

## Result

Every future scientific claim must trace from source to section, source-text record, field, draft artifact, review artifact and publication artifact.

## Restrictions Preserved

- No claim without source is acceptable.
- No draft object may be used in runtime.
- No unresolved field may be treated as validated.

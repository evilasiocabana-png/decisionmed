# 615 - ADR Patient-First Safety and Coverage Program

## Objective

Register the architectural authorization for the P0-P5 safety and coverage
program requested by the founder.

## Decision

ADR `governance/adr/docs_adr/0050_PATIENT_FIRST_SAFETY_AND_COVERAGE_PROGRAM.md`
is accepted as the governing decision for Program 27.

## Acceptance

- restoration point exists before implementation;
- patient-first identity is preserved;
- every priority has an independent test and audit gate;
- scientific gaps are not promoted without formal review;
- autonomous diagnosis and prescription remain prohibited.

## Declaration Final

Mission 615 authorizes planning and small, sequential implementation missions for
Program 27 within the limits of ADR 0050.
